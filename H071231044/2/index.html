HUNK

Grim Reaper

HUNK is the code-name of an Umbrella Security Service operator, who was the leader of the ill-fated Alpha Team in the employment of Umbrella. Cold, silent, and devoid of emotion, he is ruthless.

Personality

Almost nothing is known about HUNK's history, even his real name. The earliest piece of information relating to him was that he received training at the Military Training Center on Rockfort Island in 1996.

In only two years, HUNK proceeded to carry out a large number of successful operations, many of which he was the only survivor, earning him the nickname "Grim Reaper".
